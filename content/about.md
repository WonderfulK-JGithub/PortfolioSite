## About Me

My name is K-J, short for Karl-Johan. I am a game programmer with experience in making games with c# in untiy and c++, currently studiyng at **The Game Assembly** and looking for an internship.

When I got into game development as a experimental hobby as a teenager, it was a fun experience. When making games, seeing how a game develops peice by peice felt like a behind the scenes look into how games were made. I always looked forward to seeing my friends playing the small games I would make.

Since joining TGA I have also started liking the group aspect of making games. Solving problems together feels more rewarding than doing it by yourself. I would say my roll in a team envirnoment is someone who tries to help other people when they have blockers. It's because of this that I have focused alot on making tools for our engine.

Outside of school I have participated in various game jams on itch.io, most recently being the GMTK Game Jam 2025. For me, game jams are a nice exercise for comming up with creative game ideas. I also enjoy the reactions and feedback I get from other participants.

Other than playing and programming video games, I have been active in Nacka Församling's church, being a youth leader for 3 years and working full time as a youth assistant for 1 year. My time there helped me greatly with socialicing with people and being confortable in a leader position.
