About Me

My name is K-J, short for Karl-Johan. I am a game programmer with experience in making games with c# in untiy and c++. My main focus right now is tools & UI programming, but I enyoy all aspects of game programming. I am currently studiyng at The Game Assembly and looing for an internship.

Ever since I played Lego Star Wars as a 5 year old kid, video games have always been apart of my life. I started of playing games on Nintendo consoles,mainly Mario & Zelda. Once I got my first PC, I starded playing games in a veriety of genres, such as CS2, Stardew Valley, League of Legends and the Final Fantasy series of games.

Video games were my main hobby until when I was about 15, which was the time I got into programming. After trying out game programming at a summer camp, I started working on hobby projects in Game Maker Studio. Since I found it interesting, I choose to study game development in Highschool at LBS Stockholm Södra.

After higschool, and a gap year working as a Youth Assistant at church, I started studying game programming at The Game Assembly. Studying at TGA has been a blast, especially the group projects. The game projects have felt well coordinated between diciplince and everyone in my groups committed and wants to make the best possible game.

Outside of school, I have been active in church in Nacka Församling since 2019, both as voluntaring youth leader and part & full-time work. My time there has made me great at socializing with different kinds of people and has given me great leadership experience.

I have also participated in many online game jams on itch.io over the years, including the 3 latest GMTK game jams. It’s something I enjoy doing when there is time for it.
